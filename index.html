About Us

Who Are We?

Digital Minds was founded in 2007. It at first wasn't successful, but after another attempt at starting up, Digital Minds skyrocketed.

Digital Minds is a website development agency. It helps businesses, contractors, and other individuals meet their development needs, whether it is research, coding help, or SEO tips.

Our agency can assist you by providing various online courses to help with your coding, and achieve your relevant qualifications. We currently have online courses in HTML, Javascript, and our own specialised Python course. We encourage you to check them out and leave feedback on our social media.

We are different from the other agencies as we truly value our customer service and strive to make better websites. We believe that a happy customer makes a happy website and aim to do our best!
